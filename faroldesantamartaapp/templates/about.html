About Farol de Santa Marta

The "Praia Limpa Santa Marta" project is dedicated to protecting and preserving the beautiful beaches of Farol de Santa Marta. Our mission is to engage the local community and tourists in conservation efforts, ensuring that this stunning coastline remains pristine for future generations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to protect and enhance the coastal and marine environments around Farol de Santa Marta through community engagement, conservation initiatives, and educational programs. We believe that by working together, we can ensure that future generations will be able to enjoy the pristine beaches and vibrant ecosystems that make this region so special.

Our Vision

We envision a future where the natural beauty of Farol de Santa Marta is preserved and cherished by all. Our vision is to create a sustainable model of environmental stewardship that inspires individuals, communities, and organizations to take action in protecting the world's coastlines and oceans.

Our Values

  • Environmental Stewardship: We are dedicated to the responsible management and care of our natural resources.
  • Community Engagement: We believe in the power of collective action and work closely with local communities to drive change.
  • Education and Awareness: We are committed to educating the public about the importance of environmental conservation.
  • Sustainability: We strive to implement practices that ensure long-term environmental health and resilience.
  • Transparency: We operate with openness and integrity, ensuring that our supporters know exactly how their contributions are making a difference.
