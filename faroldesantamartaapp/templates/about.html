About Us

The "Praia Limpa Santa Marta" project is dedicated to protecting and preserving the beautiful beaches of Farol de Santa Marta. Our mission is to engage the local community and tourists in conservation efforts, ensuring that this stunning coastline remains pristine for future generations.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, "Praia Limpa Santa Marta" was born out of a passion for environmental conservation and a love for the natural beauty of Farol de Santa Marta. Our founders, a group of dedicated environmentalists and community members, recognized the need for a concerted effort to address the growing pollution and littering issues caused by increased tourism.
